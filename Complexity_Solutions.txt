GATE Complexity Solutions

Q1.
T(n)=T(n/2)+T(2n/5)+7n
Since (1/2 + 2/5)=0.9 < 1, total recursive work decreases geometrically.
Hence,
T(n)=Θ(n).

Q2.

T(n)=Θ(n log n) when:
a=b and f(n)=Θ(n).
(Equivalent form: n^(log_b a)=n and f(n)=Θ(n log^0 n).)

Q5.
Outer loop runs Θ(n) times.
Middle loop: Θ(log n).
So p=Θ(log n).
Last loop doubles k each time → Θ(log log n).
Therefore,
q = Θ(n log log n).
